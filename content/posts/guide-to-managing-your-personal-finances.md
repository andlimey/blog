Adulting is a daunting task. When I first started out, I was intimidated. There was simply so much I didn't know.

I read up a lot, and made mistakes along the way. I am writing this guide for 2 reasons:

-   To distill my thoughts
-   To help those who feel lost

The opinions I express are my own, and I am certainly no expert. This guide is meant to give you a basic overview of how you can manage the different aspects of your personal finance.

It is ultimately up to you to find out more.
